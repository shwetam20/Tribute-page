Highlights of Mumbai :

  • Arabian Sea - Mumbai is an island city located on reclaimed land surrounded by the amazing Arabian sea"
  • British Architecture - The south of Mumbai has beautiful heritage architecture which was build by the British
  • Cosmopolitan City - It is a cosmopolitan city and a melting pot of diverse people from all around India and abroad
  • Night-life - It is the city that never sleeps and has an amazing night life with clubs, restuarants and hotels
  • Bollywood - It has Bollywood which attracts cinematic talent from all over the country and even outside
  • Financial Capital - It is the financial capital of India and provides abundant opportunities to people in diverse sectors
  • Public Transport - It has the local trains called Metro and Best buses which connect all the suburbs of the city to the city center
  • Monsoons - Being a coastal city, Mumbai rainy season is the most enjoyable which is four months long.
  • Greenery - It has humid climate and lots of greenery and lovely trees and birds
  • Tourism - It has lovely food, theatre, historical monuments, beaches and places to visit in and around Mumbai
